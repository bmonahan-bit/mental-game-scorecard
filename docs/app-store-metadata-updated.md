## Description (4000 chars max)

Mental Game Scorecard is the official app of Paul Monahan Golf — built around Paul's proven mental performance framework from The Most Important Game.

Track your mental game alongside your strokes. Every hole, log which mental strengths (Heroes) showed up and which mental traps (Bandits) crept in. After 18 holes, your Mental Net tells you whether your mind helped or hurt you — independent of your score.

HEROES AND BANDITS
Paul's framework identifies five Heroes that unlock your best golf: Love, Acceptance, Commitment, Vulnerability, and Grit. They each directly counter a Bandit: Fear, Frustration, Doubt, Shame, and Quit. The matchup grid makes it easy to log both in seconds after each hole.

MENTAL NET
Mental Net = Heroes minus Bandits. A positive number means your mind was an asset. Track it across rounds to see whether your mental game is genuinely improving.

PRE-ROUND ROUTINE
A guided mental preparation before every round. Set your intention, check your energy and sleep levels, and get focused before you tee it up.

IN-GAME CADDIE
Enable the Caddie for hole-by-hole mental coaching. After each hole, the Caddie reads your active Bandits and Heroes and delivers a targeted tip — helping you reset and refocus in real time.

POST-ROUND REFLECTION
Three questions after every round: What mental habits should you keep? What should you stop? What will you start next round? Your answers are saved and tied to your round data.

DASHBOARD AND HISTORY
See your Mental Net trend over time, which Heroes you rely on, which Bandits keep appearing, and how your mental game correlates with your sleep quality and energy level. Identify patterns you can't see hole-by-hole.

COURSE INTEGRATION
Search 40,000+ courses worldwide. The app auto-fills par and yardage for every hole on your selected tee — or enter them manually. Track FIR, GIR, and putts alongside your mental data.

FREE ACCOUNT
Play 3 rounds free with no account required. Create a free profile to back up your rounds to the cloud, sync across devices, and receive personalized coaching insights from Paul based on your actual Heroes and Bandits data.

FOR COACHES
Coaches can create a roster, share their coach code with students, and see each player's mental performance data — top Hero, top Bandit, average Mental Net, win rate, and recent round history.

## App Store Review Notes
The app allows 3 free rounds before requiring a free account (email only — no payment).

To access the admin dashboard, navigate to the app URL and add /#admin to the URL. You will be prompted for a PIN.

The app uses the following third-party services:
- Clerk (authentication — Apple Sign In, Google, email)
- Convex (cloud database for user profiles)
- Golf Course API (course search — read only)
- Open-Meteo (weather — no personal data sent)
- Sentry (error monitoring — no PII collected)

No payment is ever required. No subscription. The app is completely free after creating a free account.

Apple Sign In is available on the sign-up screen alongside Google and email.
