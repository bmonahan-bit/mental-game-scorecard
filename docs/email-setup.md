# support@mentalgamescorecard.com — Setup Guide

## Why this email must exist before App Store submission
Apple requires a working support email on the App Store listing.
They will send review communications to this address.
Users contact it for account deletion requests (legally required).

## Option 1 — Google Workspace (recommended, $6/month)
1. Go to workspace.google.com
2. Sign up with domain mentalgamescorecard.com (or use paulmonahan.com)
3. Create user: support@mentalgamescorecard.com
4. Verify domain ownership via DNS record
5. Done — full Gmail interface, works on iPhone

## Option 2 — Forwarder (free, simplest)
If you own mentalgamescorecard.com domain:
1. Log into your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.)
2. Go to Email Forwarding
3. Create forward: support@mentalgamescorecard.com → your personal email
4. All support emails arrive in your existing inbox
5. Reply from your personal email (less professional but works)

## Option 3 — Netlify Email (if domain on Netlify)
Netlify doesn't offer email hosting — use Option 1 or 2.

## Domain status
If mentalgamescorecard.com is not yet registered:
- Register at namecheap.com (~$12/year)
- Then set up forwarding (Option 2, free) or Google Workspace (Option 1)

## What to do with support emails
- Account deletion requests: delete from Convex dashboard + Clerk dashboard
- Data export requests: export from Convex dashboard
- General support: answer within 24 hours (Apple monitors response time)

## Auto-responder recommended
Set up an auto-reply:
"Thanks for contacting Mental Game Scorecard support.
We aim to respond within 24 hours Monday through Friday.
For account deletion, include 'Delete My Account' in the subject line."
